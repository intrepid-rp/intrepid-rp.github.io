wetlands Heritage

Life in the bogs, swamps, or marshes of the wetlands revolves around the waterways. Activities like fishing, gathering resources, and trading, dominate life in your community. You value simple things in life that others take for granted, such as the warmth of a fire, a dry place to sleep, and drinkable water. Your resilience and resourcefulness are invaluable assets as you venture beyond the wetlands.

Waterbourne. You gain proficiency with water vehicles. You have advantage on checks related to operating and maneuvering water vehicles in wetland environments.

Marsh Lore. You gain proficiency in the Nature skill. You have advantage on checks to identify plants, animals, and natural features found in wetlands. This knowledge is essential for survival and thriving in these unique environments.

Languages. You know Common and one additional language, typically Goblin or Ophidian.

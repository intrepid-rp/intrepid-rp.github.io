Netherdeep

The Netherdeep is a vast and perilous region filled with fissures, caves, tunnels, and immense caverns. It's residents, collectively referred to as "Netherlings", build robust communities amidst underground rivers, lakes, magma flows, and colossal stalactites and stalagmites. Life here is dangerous, with settlements maintained through constant patrols and vigilance, showcasing the resilience and adaptability of its inhabitants. Above, the Shallows serve as the safest layer where Netherling farmers, traders, and guards come into contact with surface-dwelling "Brightlings".

Vigilant. Let's please update Vigilant: You gain proficiency in the Perception skill. You add your PB when rolling initiative in the Netherdeep.

Wayfinder. You have an innate ability to find pathways between two points, map routes in three dimensions, and navigate by landmarks based on sight, smell, or sound. You gain advantage on checks to navigate and map the complex tunnels and passages of the Netherdeep.

Languages. You know Netherese and one other language. Common, Dwarvish, and Elvish are the most common options.

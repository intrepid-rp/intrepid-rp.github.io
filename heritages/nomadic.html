Nomadic Heritage

Nomadic heritage folks come from close-knit communities that frequently travel between areas. They might move with the seasons, follow trade routes, or have their own unique reasons for roaming. These people are well-equipped for travel and understand the environment and its hazards or benefits.

Resilient. You have advantage on checks or saves made to resist debilitating weather effects, such as those caused by extreme heat or cold. Once per long rest, you can reduce your exhaustion level by one when completing a short rest.

Traveler. You have proficiency in the Survival skill.

Languages. You know Common and one additional language of your choice, depending on the regions your people frequent or the kinds of communities your people trade with. Dwarvish and Elvish are most common.

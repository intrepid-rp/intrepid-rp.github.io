Wildlands Heritage

Wildlands heritage characters grow up in wild areas filled with powerful natural magic, possibly from a connection to the fey realms or ancient spells. This magic changes the animals, making them wise and able to speak. They form nature-based societies different from humanoid ones, where beastkin and humanoids live alongside one another in harmony. Members of these communities protect their way of life. When encroaching civilization threatens, they send out their more humanoid members to gather information or negotiate.

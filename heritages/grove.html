Grove Heritage

Grove heritage characters were raised in the values of grove elf communities. When elves first appeared, many settled in lush forests, becoming the first grove elves. They built communities that lived in harmony with nature, not trying to control it. These elves protect their forests, worshiping in sacred groves and defending their land against intruders. As cities expand and natural areas shrink, many creatures now share the grove elves' values. Anyone who respects the wild is welcome to settle among them.

Canopy Walker. You have a climbing speed equal to your walking speed.

Elf Weapon Training. You have proficiency with the longsword, shortsword, shortbow, and longbow.

Languages. You know Common and one additional language of your choice, with Elvish being the most popular option.

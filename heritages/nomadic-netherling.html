Nomadic Netherling

You hail from the deepest and most terrible part of the Netherdeep where bands of nomadic Netherlings rarely stay in one place longer than it takes to sleep between hunting beasts, gathering edible fungus or moss, and avoiding monsters. Life-sustaining resources are rare and the monsters are terrifying, but the bonds between Netherling nomads are powerful.

Forager. You are proficient with the herbalism kit. You double your PB when using a herbalism kit underground.

Survivalist. You are proficient in the Survival skill. You double your PB when using the Survival skill underground.

Languages. You know Netherese and one other language. Common, Dwarvish, and Elvish are the most common options.

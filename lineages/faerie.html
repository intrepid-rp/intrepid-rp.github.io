Faerie

Age. You have a lifespan of around one century.

Size. Faeries are Medium-sized creatures. Pixies, a diminutive type of Faerie, are considered Small.

Flight. You have a flying speed equal to your walking speed as long as you are not wearing heavy armor. Wearing medium armor or carrying a heavy load (such as a creature of your size) while flying reduces your speed by half.

Fey Ancestry. You have advantage on saves against being charmed, and magic can't put you to sleep. You can instantly make a flower blossom, a seed pod open, or a leaf bud bloom.

Sly. You gain proficiency in the Stealth skill.

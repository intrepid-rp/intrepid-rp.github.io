Aqualan

Age. Aqualans mature at the same rate as humans with a similar lifespan.

Size. Similar in size to humans, Aqualans vary widely in height and weight. Your size is Medium.

Aquatic. Your webbed hands and feet give you a swim speed equal to your walking speed. You can hold your breath for up to 20 minutes.

Marine Bond. You possess a deep connection with sea creatures, granting you advantage on checks to charm and befriend aquatic animals.

Tidal Grace. Your movements are fluid and graceful, like the ebb and flow of the tide. You have proficiency in the Acrobatics skill.

Goblin

Age. Goblins reach adulthood at around age 12 and live up to 60 years.

Size. Goblins are 3 to 4 feet tall and weigh between 40 and 80 pounds. Your size is Small.

Elusive. You can Disengage or Hide as a bonus action on your turn.

Fierce. You exhibit a surprising amount of strength and tenacity when backed into a corner. When you are surprised, or in the first round of combat in which your initiative is 10 or less, you gain a +1 bonus to weapon attacks and damage.

Resourceful. You are proficient with improvised weapons.

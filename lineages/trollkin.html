Trollkin

Age. Trolls reach maturity in their early teens. Their regenerative nature extends their lifespans, commonly living to over 100 years old.

Size. You are tall and lanky, standing between 7 and 8 feet tall. Your size is Medium.

Regeneration. As a bonus action on your turn, you can expend one hit die to regain HP. This ability does not work if you have taken fire or acid damage since the end of your last turn. You can use this ability a number of times equal to your PB per long rest.

Robust Gut. You have advantage on CON saves against poison, from ingesting distasteful substances, or from inhaling unpleasant smells such as the Stinking Cloud spell.

Claws. You are proficient in using your claws to make unarmed strikes that deal 1d6 + STR slashing damage.

Celestial

Age. You reach adulthood by your 20th year and live up to 750 years old.

Size. You are Medium or Small.

Radiant Soul. You know the Dancing Lights cantrip and you are resistant to radiant damage.

Darkvision. You have darkvision to a range of 60 feet.

Divine Healing. Once per long rest, you can touch a creature causing them to regenerate hit points equal to 1d4 times your WIS (minimum 1).

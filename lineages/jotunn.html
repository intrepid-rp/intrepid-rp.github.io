Jotunn

Age. Jotunn reach adulthood around the age of 20 and live into their mid 100s.

Size. Adults are between 7 and 8 feet tall and weigh between 250 and 350 pounds. Your size is Medium.

Powerful. You are proficient in the Athletics skill and you count as Large when determining the weight you can carry, push, drag, or lift.

Sturdy. You have advantage in saving throws against being knocked prone.

Strong Grip. You can wield two-handed or heavy weapons in a single hand.

Ophidian

Age. Ophidians reach adulthood in their late teenage years and rarely live to be more than 100 years old.

Size. Most Ophidians are about the same height as humans but are more slender, weighing around. Your size is Medium. If you choose to play a character shorter than 4 feet, your size is Small.

Speed. Your base swimming speed is equal to your walking speed (30 feet).

Darkvision. You have darkvision to a range of 60 feet.

Natural Weapons. You can use your fangs as natural weapons to make unarmed strikes that deal 1d6 + your CON modifier of poison damage.

Poison Resilience. You have advantage on saves against becoming poisoned, and you are resistant to poison damage.

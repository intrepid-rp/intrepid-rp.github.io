Gnome

Gnomes are small elf-like people with pointy ears, gleaming eyes, and mischievous grins. They typically have large noses and, unlike elves, their men grow beards.

Age. Gnomes reach adulthood in their late teenage years and can live about 350 to 500 years.

Size. Gnome adults are between 3 and 4 feet tall and weigh around 45 pounds. Your size is Small.

Cunning. You have advantage on saving throws against being charmed or other types of mind control.

Inquisitive. You gain proficiency with the Investigation skill.

Crafty. You gain proficiency with one tool of your choice.

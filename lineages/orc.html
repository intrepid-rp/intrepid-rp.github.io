Orc

Age. Orcs reach adulthood in their mid teenage years and rarely live to be more than 100 years old.

Size. Male and female orcs generally stand 6 to 7 feet tall and weigh around 250 pounds. Your size is Medium.

Tenacious. When you fail an attack roll, your next successful attack deals extra damage equal to your proficiency bonus.

Fierce. Your naturally powerful build gives you proficiency in one of the following skills: Acrobatics, Animal Handling, Athletics, or Intimidation.

Explosive. You can jump twice as far or high as other Medium creatures.

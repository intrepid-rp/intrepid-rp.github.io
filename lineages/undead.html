Undead

These skeletons, zombies, or ghostlike beings have been brought back to life, either intentionally or through an unexpected mystical process. Seeking redemption, driven by curiosity, or striving to recover the memories of their past, they are easily lured to lives of exploration.

Age. You are the same age as you were at death. You do not age.

Size. You are Small or Medium depending on your lineage in life.

Previous Life. Choose one trait from your lineage in life. For example, if you were originally a Halfling you could choose the Brave trait.

Undead Fortitude. You add your PB to death saving throws and you do not need to eat, drink, or breathe.

Relentless. Once per long rest when you are reduced to zero HP, you can drop to 1 HP instead.

Machinari

Age. Your nexus, the power core which houses your soul, lasts up to 500 years but may fail sooner without maintenance or replacement of the mechanical parts with which it integrates.

Size. Machinari are built to represent other lineages. Your size is generally Medium or Small.

Mechanical. You don't eat, drink, or breathe. You are immune to poison. Your nexus requires a normal amount of rest each day and you are able to dream.

Precise Recall. You can remember anything you've experienced in the last month with perfect accuracy.

Tinkerer. You are proficient with Tinker's Tools.

Kobold

Age. Young kobolds grow quickly, reaching adulthood in their early teens, with a lifespan of around 80 years.

Size. Kobolds are generally around 3 feet tall. Your size is Small.

Darkvision. You have darkvision to a range of 60 feet.

Breath Weapon. You gain a breath weapon that does one type of damage depending on your draconic ancestry. Choose from Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Poison damage. Creatures within a 15 ft line must make a DEX save or take 1d8 damage. The damage increases to 2d8 at 6th level, 3d8 at 11th level, and 4d8 at 16th level.

Curious. You are naturally drawn to crafting objects and figuring out how they work. You gain proficiency with one tool of your choice.

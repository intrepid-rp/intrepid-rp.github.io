Kitsune

Age. A kitsune reaches adulthood in their late teens and rarely lives longer than a century.

Size. Your kitsune and humanoid form are the same size and build. Your size is Small or Medium, depending on the humanoid form you choose (human or halfling).

Shapechanger. Your true shape is that of a fox-like humanoid but you can use your action to change between two additional forms: a fox, and a specific human or halfing. When you change into your fox form, your gear magically changes shape with you.

Deceptive. You are proficient in the deception skill and have advantage on skill checks to hide your shapshifting nature.

Beastspeak. When in your fox form, you can communicate with beasts as if under the effect of the Speak with Animals spell.

Sailor

Whether you're navigating merchant routes, serving as a privateer, or sailing under the black flag, the sea is your home. You've braved storms, battled sea monsters, fought off pirates, and sailed to far-off lands. With skills honed in navigation, ship maintenance, and weather prediction, you're driven by adventure and the mysteries that lie beyond the horizon.

Skill Proficiencies: Choose two from Acrobatics, Perception, Nature, or Survival.

Other Proficiencies: You are proficient with water vehicles. You also choose one set of tools related to your job on ship, for example: Navigator Tools for a quartermaster or helmsman, Construction Tools for the carpenter, Provisioner Tools for the cook or steward, Medical Kit for the ship's surgeon/medic, or Clothier Tools for the salemaker. Alternatively, you can choose a gaming set or musical instrument.

Equipment: A set of common clothes; one set of tools, gaming set, or musical instrument; a scroll tube with maps of coastlines you've visited; 50ft rope and grappling hook; mess kit; bedroll; a belt pouch with 10 gp.

Talent: Choose one from Artillerist, Hand to Hand, or Polyglot.

Gladiator

You honed your skills in the harsh arenas of combat, fighting for survival and glory. Your strength, agility, and showmanship captivate audiences and strike fear into your enemies. Scars from countless battles serve as a testament to your resilience. Driven by a desire for freedom and fame, you thrive on the adrenaline of the fight and the roar of the crowd.

Skill Proficiencies: Choose two from Acrobatics, Athletics, Intimidation, and Performance.

Other Proficiencies: You have a signature melee weapon of your choice, adding +1d4 damage with it. Also choose between Healer's kit, gaming set, or additional language.

Equipment: Your signature melee weapon, common clothes, bedroll, waterskin, mementos from previous victories, and a pouch containing 10 gp.

Talent: Choose one from Armored Combatant, Combat Conditioning, Hand to Hand, or Two Weapon Mastery.

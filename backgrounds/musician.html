Musician

Your life has been a symphony of melodies and rhythms. From grand halls to tavern corners, you've enchanted audiences with your music. With a deep understanding of various instruments, you compose and perform pieces that stir the soul. Driven by a passion for harmony and creativity, you travel far and wide, seeking inspiration and sharing your art. Your talents not only entertain but also uplift spirits and forge connections.

Skill Proficiencies: Choose two from Insight, Perception, Persuasion, and Performance.

Other Proficiencies: You are proficient with three musical instruments.

Equipment: A set of common clothes and fine clothes, two musical instruments, a saddlebag with supplies to keep your instrument in top condition (e.g., strings, reeds, tuning fork, etc.), a songbook, a letter of recommendation from a patron or guild, and a belt pouch with 10 gp.

Talent: Choose one from Polyglot, Trade Skills, and Touch of Luck.

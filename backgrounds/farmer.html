Farmer

You've spent countless hours working under the sun, gaining practical knowledge of nature, plants, animals, and the weather. Though not formally educated, your hands-on experience has made you resilient and resourceful. You understand the rhythms of life and the land, enduring the hard, dirty work with a hearty spirit. You bring this grounded wisdom and strength to your new adventures.

Skill Proficiencies: Choose two from Animal Handling, Nature.

Other Proficiencies: Choose two from Farmer's Implements, Herbalism Kit, Land Vehicles, or Weaver's Tools.

Equipment: Common or working clothes, hat, a farm implement (such as a sickle, shearing scissors, or spade), herbalism kit, and a pouch containing 10 gp.

Talent: Choose one from Athletic, Physical Fortitude, or Trade Skills.

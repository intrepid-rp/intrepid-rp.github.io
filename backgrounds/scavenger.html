Scavenger

Years of scavenging for concealed treasures and discarded, yet useful items have honed your keen eye for hidden treasures and their value. Driven by curiosity and necessity, you thrive in navigating ruins, repairing useful items, and finding scraps of food to survive. You have built a network of traders, collectors, and merchants, or you generally know where to find them.

Skill Proficiencies: Choose two from History, Investigation, Insight, or Survival.

Other Proficiencies: Choose two from a gaming set, musical instrument, cook's utensils, or tinker tools.

Equipment: A set of common clothes, a pry bar, a sack with up to 5gp of scavenged items that you have repaired, and a belt pouch with 10 gp.

Talent: Choose one from Aware, Dungeoneer, or Physical Fortitude.

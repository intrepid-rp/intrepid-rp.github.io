Criminal

Your life of crime has imbued you with street smarts and a network of underworld contacts. You excel in covert operations and are invaluable in situations requiring subtlety and cunning but your past attracts enemies and unresolved debts, adding peril to your adventures.

Skill Proficiencies: Choose two from Stealth, Investigation, Insight, or Deception.

Additional Proficiencies: You know Thieves' Cant. If you already know this language, you learn a different language of your choice. You gain proficiency with a tool and a vehicle.

Equipment: Five pieces of chalk, a grappling hook, a set of dark traveler's clothes or a costume, and a pouch containing 10 gp.

Talent: Choose one from Covert, Scrutinous, or Touch of Luck.

Outcast

You've led a tough life as a petty criminal, unfortunate victim, or neglected child surviving on the fringes of society. Scraping by in harsh city streets has made you adept at surviving, even thriving, in the shadows. Maybe you're a street urchin, a pickpocket, or a former bandit seeking redemption. Regardless, the challenges and adventures of living outside the law have shaped you into someone who can handle any dark situation.

Skill Proficiencies: Choose two from Deception, Insight, Sleight of Hand, or Stealth.

Other Proficiencies: One type of game set and one of the following: a disguise kit, a poisoner’s kit, or thieves’ tools.

Equipment: A dark cloak and a set of dark common clothes, a silver coin given to you by a kind stranger, and a pouch containing 10 gp.

Talent: Choose one from Aware, Covert, or Hand to Hand.

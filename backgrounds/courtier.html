Courtier

As a courtier, you have gained access to noble circles through your upper-class family, friendships, or talents. Your aptitude for navigating complex bureaucracies and forming strategic friendships has made you indispensable in court. You know precisely whom to approach for favors or secrets. Your talent for gathering and leveraging gossip ensures you remain a pivotal, if discreet, figure in the noble landscape.

Skill Proficiencies: Choose two from History, Insight, Performance, or Persuasion.

Additional Proficiencies: One language and one tool proficiency from Painter's Supplies, Calligrapher's Supplies or a musical instrument.

Equipment: A set of fine clothes, a letter of introduction from a noble house, a pouch containing 10 gp.

Talent: Choose one from Mental Fortitude, Polyglot, or Scrutinous.

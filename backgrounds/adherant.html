Adherant

You devoted yourself to a singular purpose, whether it's a faith, belief, or cause. If you served a deity, priests likely assigned you daily prayers, holy rituals, and reading sacred texts. If you were part of a secret society, you might have participated in secretive ceremonies or initiations. Following these strict routines has instilled a deep sense of duty and meaning in you, understood by only a few.

Skill Proficiencies: Choose two from History, Investigation, Religion, or Persuasion.

Other Proficiencies: Calligrapher's supplies and one type of tool or instrument of your choice.

Equipment: A prayer book or ceremonial dagger, a reliquary or holy symbol, a block of fragrant incense, vestments, common clothes, and a pouch containing 10 gp.

Talent: Choose one from Field Medic, Mental Fortitude, or Ritualist.

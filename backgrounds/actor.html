Actor

A master of role-playing, improvisation, deception, and adapting to diverse situations, your life on stage has helped you hone the ability to navigate the unpredictable landscapes of adventuring life. Perhaps you're a performer turned adventurer seeking fame, a con artist seeking wealth, or a spy infiltrating enemy ranks. But beneath the masks lies a complex individual, searching for identity amidst the roles.

Skill Proficiencies: Choose two from Deception, Performance, Persuasion, Sleight of Hand.

Other Proficiencies: Disguise kit, and one type of gaming set or instrument of your choice.

Equipment: Two costumes, a disguise kit, a set of fine clothes, and a belt pouch with 10 gp.

Talent: Choose one from Aware, Mental Prowess, or Polyglot.

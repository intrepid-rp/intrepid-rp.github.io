Hermit

As a hermit, you've spent years in seclusion, either within the quiet walls of a monastery or entirely alone in the wild. This isolation has given you a deep sense of self-reliance and inner peace. Your unique experiences have honed your wisdom and survival skills, making you an adept problem-solver and a calm presence in any situation.

Skill Proficiencies: Choose two from Medicine, Nature, Religion, Survival.

Other Proficiencies: Choose two from a gaming set, musical instrument, cook's utensils, herbalism kit, or woodcarver's tools.

Equipment: Common clothes, quilt, herbalism kit, and a pouch containing 10 gp.

Talent: Choose one from Covert, Field Medic, or Physical Fortitude.

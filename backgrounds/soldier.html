Soldier

You've dedicated a big part of your life to protecting others, whether in the military or defending your community. This demanding job requires intense training, discipline, and giving up comforts many people enjoy. Maybe you're a veteran seeking a new direction, a deserter fleeing the horrors of war, or a passionate newcomer looking for different ways to serve your cause. Whatever your path, the sense of duty you carry never fades.

Skill Proficiencies: Choose two from Animal Handling, Athletics, Medicine, or Survival.

Other Proficiencies: Land or water vehicles, and one tool of your choice.

Equipment: A symbol of rank (like a letter, badge, or identification tags), a mess kit, a pack of playing cards or a set of dice, a set of common clothes, and a pouch containing 10 gp.

Talent: Choose one from Combat Casting, Combat Conditioning, or Field Medic.

Apothecary

You have learned the ancient art of potion-making and herbal remedies, skillfully blending chemistry, botany, and medicine to combine natural ingredients into powerful elixirs (or even poisons). You provided your invaluable services from your own shop, a remote hut, or at court. Your curiosity, resourcefulness, wisdom, and healing abilities distinguish you as a remarkable adventurer.

Skill Proficiencies: Choose two from Insight, Medicine, Nature, or Survival.

Other Proficiencies: You are proficient with Herbalist Tools and Healer's Kits.

Equipment: A set of common clothes and fine clothes, a leather satchel for herbs and other supplies, Herbalist Tools, a Healer's Kit, a small knife, a bandolier to keep tools and vials within reach, a small journal for documenting your discoveries, and a belt pouch with 10 gp.

Talent: Choose one from Field Medic, Ritualist (must have spellcasting), and Trade Skills.

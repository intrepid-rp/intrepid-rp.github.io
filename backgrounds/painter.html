Painter

You find solace in the quiet of your studio, amidst the beautiful mess of brushes and paint. Through your pursuit of success as an artist, you have developed a sharp eye for detail and nuanced understanding of the world.

Skill Proficiencies: Choose two from Insight, History, Nature, or Religion.

Other Proficiencies: Painter's supplies and one additional language of your choice.

Equipment: Case of painter's supplies, painter's coveralls, common clothes, and a belt pouch with 10 gp.

Talent: Choose one from Quick, Scrutinous, or Trade Skills.

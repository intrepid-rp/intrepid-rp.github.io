Stablehand

Your life has been immersed in caring for animals. Raised in stables, you excel in calming creatures and possess basic knowledge of medicine or herbalism. Whether soothing nervous animals, finding the best mounts and stabling for the lowest price, or concocting basic remedies, your bond with animals and healing prowess make you a welcome member of most adventuring parties.

Skill Proficiencies: Choose two from Animal Handling, Insight, Medicine, and Nature.

Other Proficiencies: Land vehicles and one of the following: herbalism kit, healer's kit, or an instrument.

Equipment: A bit and bridle, a saddlebag, two rations of animal feed, a set of common clothes, and a pouch containing 10 gp.

Talent: Choose one from Field Medic, Hand to Hand, Opportunist.
